Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to delete your ManaQuests account and what happens to your data.

Before You Delete

Account deletion is permanent. All your data including missions, XP, achievements, streaks, and connections will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Option 1: Delete from the App

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the ManaQuests app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Enter your password to confirm
  5. Tap Delete My Account

Your account will enter a 14-day grace period. You can restore it by logging in during this time. After 14 days, all data is permanently deleted.

Option 2: Request via Email

Email Deletion Request

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to the address below
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete My Account"
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 days
Email: privacy@manaquests.com

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, date of birth)
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)
  • All mission history and progress
  • XP, achievements, and streaks
  • Family and friend connections
  • All activity data

What We May Retain

In accordance with our Privacy Policy, we may retain certain anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you, as well as any data required for legal compliance.
